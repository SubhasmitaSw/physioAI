About physioAI

Hey there! Welcome to physioAI, developed by team nerdHerds.

PhysioAI is a visual recognition system influenced by several Machine Learning models. This initiative seeks to assist individuals in practising physiotherapy sessions under the supervision of AI-trained experts.

physioAI uses Pose.Net, developed by Google’s TensorFlow, and ml5.js, a JavaScript library for Machine Learning on the browser.
